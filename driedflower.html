Dried Flower Care Guide

Light & Placement

Keep out of direct sunlight to prevent fading. Display in areas with good air circulation.

Humidity Control

Avoid humid areas. Silica gel packets can help absorb moisture in display cases.

Cleaning

Gently dust with a soft brush or use compressed air for delicate pieces.

Storage

Store in a box with tissue paper in a cool, dry place when not displayed.
