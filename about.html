Our Floral Story

Welcome to Heavenly Blooms, where passion for flora meets exquisite craftsmanship. We believe every bloom has a story, and we're here to help you tell yours.

Founded in 2023 by our master florist Eeun Ice, Heavenly Blooms began as a small neighborhood flower shop with a big dream - to bring nature's beauty into every home and occasion. What started as a single workbench in a garage has blossomed into one of the region's most beloved floral destinations, serving thousands of happy customers each year.

Our Philosophy

We believe flowers have the power to transform spaces, uplift spirits, and create meaningful connections. Each arrangement we create is infused with care, creativity, and a deep respect for nature's artistry. Our designs blend classic elegance with contemporary flair, always letting the natural beauty of our flowers shine through.

Sustainable Sourcing

We're committed to sustainable practices, sourcing 85% of our flowers from local growers who share our values. Our bouquets are wrapped in biodegradable materials, and we've eliminated floral foam from all our designs.

Handcrafted Quality

Every arrangement is personally crafted by our team of expert florists. We never mass-produce or pre-make bouquets - each creation is made to order with fresh, seasonal blooms.
